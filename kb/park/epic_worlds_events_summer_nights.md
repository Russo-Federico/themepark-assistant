# Epic Worlds — Summer Nights (Seasonal Event, Park-Wide)

## Overview

Summer Nights is Epic Worlds' park-wide summer seasonal event, running from **June 15 to August 31**. Unlike Fable Fest (a Fable World-only Halloween event) or Future World Innovation Days (a Future World-only science event), Summer Nights runs across all three themed areas at once: Future World, Adventure World, and Fable World. Guests may also hear this referred to as the "summer festival," the "night event," or the "extended hours event."

## What Changes During Summer Nights

- **Extended park hours:** the park stays open until 23:00 instead of the standard 21:00 closing time.
- **Nightly light and sound show:** held at the center hub connecting all three worlds, with performances at 21:30 and 22:30 every night of the event.
- **Special food stalls:** additional food stalls open from 19:00 in all three themed areas (Future World, Adventure World, and Fable World).

## Area-Specific Highlights During Summer Nights

Some individual attractions and shows run special Summer-Nights-only content. Full details and exact showtimes for these live in each area's daily entertainment schedule:

- **Future World:** Neon Circuit runs its "Night Run" evening light-trail overlay show — see `future_world_events_daily.md`.
- **Fable World:** an Evening Lantern Ceremony (guest participation, lantern release) is held in the Fable World town square — see `fable_world_events_daily.md`.
- **Adventure World:** no Summer-Nights-exclusive show; standard daily entertainment schedule applies — see `adventure_world_events_daily.md`.

## Notes

- Summer Nights does not require a separate event ticket — standard, Premium, Family Bundle, and Annual Pass admission all include access.
- This is distinct from Fable Fest (Halloween, Fable World only, October–November) and Future World Innovation Days (science/tech, Future World only, March), which are separate seasonal events documented in their own area folders.
