# Epic Worlds — Park Overview

## About Epic Worlds

Epic Worlds is a world-class theme park destination built around the idea that every guest deserves to step outside their reality. The park is divided into three distinct themed areas, each with its own identity, atmosphere, and cast of attractions. Whether you're a thrill-seeker, a family with young children, or a curious explorer, Epic Worlds has a world built for you.

## The Three Worlds

**Future World** sits in the eastern wing of the park. It is a tech-inspired zone where science fiction becomes science fact. Expect sleek architecture, ambient electronic soundscapes, and attractions themed around space, data, and speed. Appeals primarily to teens and adults with a moderate-to-high thrill appetite.

**Adventure World** occupies the northern section. It is the park's most high-energy zone, built around outdoor expedition and extreme environments. Rapids, mountain coasters, and jungle expeditions define the experience here. Best suited for thrill-seekers and older guests.

**Fable World** fills the western wing. It is a fairy-tale and fantasy-themed area with a warm, storybook aesthetic. The area blends low and moderate thrill experiences with high visual richness. Ideal for families and younger guests, though the headline attraction appeals to a broad age range.

## General Information

- **Opening hours:** 09:00 – 21:00 daily (extended to 23:00 on event nights)
- **Park entrance:** Main Gate, south side of the park
- **Guest Services:** Located at Main Gate and at the center hub connecting all three worlds
- **First Aid:** Available at Main Gate, Future World east corridor, and Fable World town square
- **Lockers:** Available at Main Gate and at the entrance of each themed area
- **Parking:** North and South lots, tram service available from South lot

## Rules and Guest Conduct

- Guests must follow all attraction-specific height and health requirements
- Outside food and beverages are not permitted except for guests with documented dietary needs
- Smoking is permitted only in designated areas near each area entrance
- Pets are not permitted inside the park (service animals excepted)
