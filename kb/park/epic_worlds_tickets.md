# Epic Worlds — Tickets & Pricing

## Ticket Tiers

### Standard Ticket
- **Price:** €49 adult / €35 child (ages 3–11) / free under 3
- **Includes:** Full park access to all three worlds, all standard attractions
- **Validity:** Single day, non-transferable
- **Notes:** Does not include Express Pass or special event access

### Premium Ticket
- **Price:** €79 adult / €59 child
- **Includes:** Full park access + Express Pass (one use per eligible attraction) + one complimentary meal voucher (main + drink at any quick-service restaurant)
- **Validity:** Single day, non-transferable
- **Notes:** Express Pass lanes available at 12 of 16 attractions

### Family Bundle
- **Price:** €159 for 2 adults + 2 children (additional child €25)
- **Includes:** Full park access for all members, priority boarding on all low-thrill attractions, 10% discount at all retail locations
- **Validity:** Single day, all members must enter together
- **Notes:** Does not include Express Pass. Priority boarding applies to: Data Dive, Jungle Trek, Dragon Flight, and Fable World Carousel

### Annual Pass
- **Price:** €199 adult / €149 child
- **Includes:** Unlimited visits for 12 months, 15% retail discount, free standard parking
- **Validity:** 12 months from date of first activation
- **Notes:** Blackout dates apply during peak summer weekends and special event nights

## Upgrades

- **Express Pass add-on:** €25 per person (purchasable at the gate or online)
- **Meal plan upgrade:** €18 per person (one quick-service meal per day)
- **Photo package:** €30 per group (all on-ride and roaming photographer photos for the day)

## Purchase Channels

Tickets can be purchased online at epicworlds.com (5% discount vs gate price), at the Main Gate ticket booths, or via the Epic Worlds mobile app. Group bookings (15+ guests) should contact groups@epicworlds.com for dedicated pricing.
