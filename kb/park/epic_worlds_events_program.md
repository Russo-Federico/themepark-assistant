# Epic Worlds — Events Program

## Seasonal Events

### Summer Nights (June 15 – August 31)
Extended park hours until 23:00. Nightly light and sound show at the center hub at 21:30 and 22:30. Special food stalls open from 19:00 in all three areas.

### Fable Fest (October 1 – November 2)
Fable World transforms for Halloween season. Themed decorations, costumed characters, and two exclusive scare zones (not suitable for guests under 12). Special event ticket required for scare zone access (€10 add-on).

### Future World Innovation Days (March 10 – March 16)
Week-long celebration of science and technology. Daily panel talks at the Future World amphitheatre (14:00 and 16:00). Interactive science demos near Neon Circuit. Free for all park guests.

## Daily Entertainment Schedule

### Future World
- **Neon Circuit Night Run** (light show overlay on the coaster): 20:00, 21:00 — Summer Nights only
- **Data Dive Presenter Session** (live host inside attraction): 11:00, 14:00, 17:00 daily

### Adventure World
- **Thunder Rapids Stunt Show** (waterside performance): 12:30, 15:30 daily
- **Peak Assault Speed Record Attempt** (timed run with live commentary): 13:00 Saturdays only

### Fable World
- **Dragon Flight Story Show** (pre-show narrative experience): runs continuously before each ride cycle
- **Fable World Parade** (character parade through main street): 11:00 and 18:00 daily
- **Evening Lantern Ceremony** (guest participation, lantern release): 20:45 during Summer Nights
