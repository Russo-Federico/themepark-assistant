## Story

Neon Circuit puts guests inside a data packet racing through Axiom Labs' experimental fiber-optic network. The track is embedded with reactive LED lighting that responds to the train's speed, creating a tunnel of light that shifts from blue to white to blinding white as the coaster hits its top speed of 110 km/h. The layout includes two launches, a top hat element, and a zero-gravity roll over the Future World rooftop.

## Operational Notes

- No single-rider lane
- Ride closes during thunderstorms and high winds — check the Epic Worlds app for real-time status
- **Neon Circuit Night Run** — evening light-show overlay on the coaster, running at 20:00 and 21:00 during the Summer Nights seasonal event only (see `future_world_events_daily.md`)
