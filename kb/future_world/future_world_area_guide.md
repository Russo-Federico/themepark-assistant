# Future World — Area Guide

## Overview

Future World is Epic Worlds' vision of tomorrow, today. The area is designed around the aesthetic of a near-future research campus: clean lines, glowing surfaces, ambient electronic music, and architectural details that suggest scientific progress. Attractions range from a high-speed light-trail coaster to an immersive data visualization dark ride, catering to both thrill-seekers and curious explorers.

## Atmosphere & Theme

The central narrative of Future World is that guests are visiting the headquarters of a fictional technology company, Axiom Labs, which has opened its facilities to the public. Each attraction is framed as one of Axiom Labs' flagship projects. Staff are referred to as "Lab Technicians" within the area.

## Attractions

- **Orbit Station** — Space simulation experience, moderate thrill
- **Neon Circuit** — High-speed light-trail coaster, high thrill
- **Data Dive** — Interactive dark ride, low thrill, fully accessible

## Dining

- **Axiom Canteen** — Quick-service restaurant, sci-fi themed menu, allergy-friendly options available
- **Circuit Café** — Coffee and snacks, located near Neon Circuit exit

## Shopping

- **The Lab Store** — Tech-themed merchandise, Axiom Labs branded items, exclusive Future World collectibles

## Practical Information

- Area entrance is located on the east side of the center hub
- Restrooms available at north and south ends of the area
- Quiet zone available near Data Dive for sensory-sensitive guests
- Stroller and wheelchair parking available at all attraction entrances
