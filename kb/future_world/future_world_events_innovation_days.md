# Future World — Innovation Days (Seasonal Event)

## Overview

Future World Innovation Days is a week-long seasonal event exclusive to Future World, running from **March 10 to March 16**. It celebrates science and technology, matching Future World's own tech-inspired theming. Guests may also refer to this as the "science festival," "tech week," or "STEM event." This event does not affect Adventure World or Fable World.

## What Happens During Innovation Days

- **Daily panel talks:** held at the Future World amphitheatre at **14:00 and 16:00** each day of the event.
- **Interactive science demos:** set up near the Neon Circuit coaster, running throughout the day.

## Access

- Innovation Days is **free for all park guests** with standard park admission — no special event ticket or add-on is required (unlike Fable Fest's scare zones).

## Notes

- Innovation Days is unrelated to Summer Nights (park-wide, June–August) and Fable Fest (Fable World only, October–November) — see `epic_worlds_events_summer_nights.md` and `fable_world_events_fable_fest.md` for those separate events.
- Future World's regular daily entertainment schedule (Neon Circuit Night Run, Data Dive Presenter Session) continues to run during Innovation Days — see `future_world_events_daily.md`.
