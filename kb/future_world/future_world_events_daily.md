# Future World — Daily Entertainment Schedule

## Overview

This is the daily schedule of shows and performances that take place in Future World, Epic Worlds' tech-inspired themed area. Guests may ask about this as "what's on today in Future World" or "show times." These are recurring daily/seasonal shows, separate from Future World's seasonal Innovation Days event (see `future_world_events_innovation_days.md`).

## Neon Circuit Night Run

An evening light-show overlay on the Neon Circuit coaster, running at **20:00 and 21:00**. This show runs only during the Summer Nights seasonal event (June 15 – August 31) — see `epic_worlds_events_summer_nights.md` for details on that event. See `neon_circuit.md` for full attraction details.

## Data Dive Presenter Session

A live host session inside the Data Dive attraction, running at **11:00, 14:00, and 17:00 daily**. See `data_dive.md` for full attraction details.

## Character Meet & Greet

Captain Axiom appears at Orbit Station plaza at 10:00, 13:00, and 16:00 daily — see `future_world_area_guide.md` for the full character meet & greet listing.
