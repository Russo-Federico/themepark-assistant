## Story

Guests are recruited as crew members for Axiom Labs' first deep-space simulation mission. Boarding a domed simulator pod, they experience a full orbital launch sequence, a flyby of Saturn's rings, and an emergency re-entry procedure — all while the pod tilts, vibrates, and pressurises around them. The experience ends with a soft landing broadcast on a wraparound screen, to applause from a fictional mission control team.
