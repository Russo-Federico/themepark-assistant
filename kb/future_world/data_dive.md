## Story

Guests board a slow-moving vehicle shaped like a data capsule and travel through a series of giant visualisation rooms, each representing a different type of human knowledge — climate, language, music, architecture. Along the way, they interact with touchscreen panels on their vehicle to "collect" data points, competing with other vehicles for the highest score. The finale reveals how all collected data combines into a living portrait of human civilization.
