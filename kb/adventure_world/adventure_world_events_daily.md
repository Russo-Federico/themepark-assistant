# Adventure World — Daily Entertainment Schedule

## Overview

This is the daily schedule of shows and performances that take place in Adventure World, Epic Worlds' high-energy, outdoor-expedition-themed area. Guests may ask about this as "what's on today in Adventure World" or "show times." Adventure World has no seasonal event of its own (unlike Fable World's Fable Fest or Future World's Innovation Days); its only seasonal variation is the park-wide Summer Nights event — see `epic_worlds_events_summer_nights.md`.

## Thunder Rapids Stunt Show

A waterside stunt performance held near the Thunder Rapids attraction, running at **12:30 and 15:30 daily**. See `thunder_rapids.md` for full attraction details.

## Peak Assault Speed Record Attempt

A timed speed-record run on Peak Assault with live commentary, held **Saturdays only at 13:00**. See `peak_assault.md` for full attraction details.

## Character Meet & Greet

Ranger Kova appears at the Jungle Trek entrance at 11:00 and 14:30 daily — see `adventure_world_area_guide.md` for the full character meet & greet listing.
