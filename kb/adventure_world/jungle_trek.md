## Story

Guests board a canopied expedition boat and drift through a recreated jungle ecosystem following the route of Ranger Kova's first-ever field mission. The journey passes through dense animatronic vegetation, encounters with exotic wildlife models, a hidden waterfall grotto, and a final river delta scene where Kova's field journal entries are read aloud as the boat glides back to the dock. Narrated by Ranger Kova's voice actor.

## Operational Notes

- Fully wheelchair accessible
- Longest ride duration in Adventure World — effective queue time relief for the area
- Popular with families and guests taking a break between high-thrill attractions
- Operates in light rain (covered boat canopy provides partial shelter)
