## Story

Peak Assault simulates a high-altitude mountaineering rescue mission gone spectacularly wrong. Guests are strapped into suspended seats — feet dangling — and launched up a near-vertical lift hill representing the north face of the fictional Mount Kova. The descent delivers six inversions, including a flat spin over the Adventure World rooftop and a low-to-ground cobra roll through a ravine channel. Top speed: 105 km/h.

## Operational Notes

- No single-rider lane
- Closes during high winds, rain, and thunderstorms — most weather-sensitive attraction in the park
- Timed speed record runs with live commentary every Saturday at 13:00
- Highest thrill rating in the park — not suitable for guests with heart conditions, high blood pressure, or anxiety around heights
