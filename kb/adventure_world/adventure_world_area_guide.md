# Adventure World — Area Guide

## Overview

Adventure World is the park's most physically intense zone, built around the fantasy of extreme outdoor exploration. The area aesthetic draws from mountain wilderness, jungle expeditions, and river canyon environments. Attractions are predominantly outdoor, high-capacity, and designed to deliver genuine physical sensation. It is the most visited area of Epic Worlds during peak summer periods.

## Atmosphere & Theme

The central narrative frames Adventure World as a national park managed by the fictional Kova Expedition Corps. Guests are positioned as expedition participants joining Ranger Kova's team for a series of increasingly extreme field missions. Staff are referred to as "Expedition Guides."

## Attractions

- **Thunder Rapids** — River rapids ride, high thrill
- **Peak Assault** — Suspended mountain coaster, very high thrill
- **Jungle Trek** — Slow expedition boat ride, low thrill, fully accessible

## Dining

- **Base Camp Grill** — Quick-service, grilled food menu, large outdoor seating area
- **Ranger's Flask** — Drinks and snacks kiosk near Peak Assault exit

## Shopping

- **Expedition Outfitters** — Adventure and outdoor-themed merchandise, Kova Corps branded gear

## Practical Information

- Area entrance is located on the north side of the center hub
- Water rides operate weather-permitting — Thunder Rapids may close during cold weather periods
- Guests exiting Thunder Rapids should expect to be significantly wet
- Towel and poncho rental available at the area entrance kiosk
- Stroller and wheelchair parking available at all attraction entrances
