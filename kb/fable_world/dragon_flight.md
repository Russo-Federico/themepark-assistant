## Story

Guests are invited by Lady Fable to take flight on the back of Ember, the last dragon of the Fable Realm. Seated on a hanging bench that tilts and swoops in sync with a 180-degree wraparound screen, guests soar over enchanted forests, frozen mountain passes, a kraken-filled ocean, and the glittering spires of the Fable capital city. Wind, mist, and heat effects accompany each environment. The experience ends with Ember landing in the town square of Fable World itself, seamlessly connecting the film's finale to the physical area outside.

## Operational Notes

- Highest guest-per-hour capacity in the park — a primary redirect target when Adventure World or Future World attractions reach peak wait times
- Indoor, climate-controlled — operates in all weather conditions
- Story pre-show runs continuously in the loading chamber — guests who board mid-pre-show hear the full narration before the main experience begins
- Moderate thrill profile with broad age appeal — suitable for most guests including older children and seniors
