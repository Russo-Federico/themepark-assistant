# Fable World — Daily Entertainment Schedule

## Overview

This is the daily schedule of shows, parades, and performances that take place in Fable World, Epic Worlds' fairy-tale and fantasy-themed area. Guests may ask about this as "what's on today in Fable World," "show times," or "the parade schedule." These are recurring daily shows, separate from Fable World's seasonal Fable Fest event (see `fable_world_events_fable_fest.md`).

## Dragon Flight Story Show

A pre-show narrative experience that runs continuously inside the Dragon Flight attraction, before each ride cycle. Guests who board mid-pre-show hear the full narration before the main experience begins. See `dragon_flight.md` for full attraction details.

## Fable World Parade

A character parade through Fable World's main street, running at **11:00 and 18:00 daily**.

## Evening Lantern Ceremony

A guest-participation lantern release held in the Fable World town square at **20:45**. This show runs only during the Summer Nights seasonal event (June 15 – August 31) — see `epic_worlds_events_summer_nights.md` for details on that event.

## Character Meet & Greet

Lady Fable appears in Fable World town square at 10:00, 13:00, and 17:00 daily — see `fable_world_area_guide.md` for the full character meet & greet listing.
