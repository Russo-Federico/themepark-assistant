# Fable World — Area Guide

## Overview

Fable World is Epic Worlds' most visually rich environment, designed to feel like stepping into the pages of an illustrated storybook. Cobbled streets, warm amber lighting, hand-painted facades, and a continuous ambient score drawn from orchestral fairy-tale motifs create an atmosphere unlike any other area in the park. Thrill levels range from very low to moderate, with an emphasis on story, spectacle, and shared experience.

## Atmosphere & Theme

The area is built around the fictional mythology of the Fable Realm, a land where legendary creatures and human kingdoms coexist. Lady Fable, the area's central character, serves as the guests' narrator and guide. Staff are referred to as "Storytellers."

## Attractions

- **Dragon Flight** — Flying theatre, moderate thrill
- **Fable World Carousel** — Classic carousel, no thrill (family)

## Dining

- **The Gilded Goose** — Sit-down restaurant, fairy-tale themed menu, advance reservation recommended
- **Fable Bites** — Quick-service snacks and sweets kiosk in the town square

## Shopping

- **Lady Fable's Emporium** — Storybook-themed merchandise, plush characters, exclusive Fable World artwork prints

## Practical Information

- Area entrance is located on the west side of the center hub
- Evening lantern ceremony (Summer Nights) takes place in the Fable World town square at 20:45
- Fable World Parade departs from the area's main street at 11:00 and 18:00 daily
- Quietest area of the park — recommended for guests seeking a lower-stimulation environment
