# Fable World — Accessibility Guide

## Wheelchair & Mobility Aid Access

Fable World is fully wheelchair accessible. All pathways are cobbled-effect but designed with smooth inlaid accessibility lanes running through the centre of each street, clearly marked with amber accessibility symbols consistent with the area's visual theme.

### Attraction-Specific Access

**Dragon Flight**
- Wheelchair transfer required to board the flying theatre bench seat
- Transfer assistance provided by Storytellers at the boarding gate
- The theatre chamber itself is accessible by wheelchair up to the transfer point
- Companion seating adjacent to transfer point
- Not recommended for guests with severe claustrophobia (enclosed pre-show chamber)

**Fable World Carousel**
- Fully accessible — stationary carriage available for wheelchair users who prefer not to transfer
- No transfer required for stationary carriage option
- Companion seating available in stationary carriage

## Sensory Considerations

Fable World is the park's lowest-stimulation area overall. Specific notes:

- Dragon Flight pre-show involves dim lighting and dramatic orchestral audio — a sensory preview is available on request at the attraction entrance
- Quiet zone available in the garden area behind The Gilded Goose restaurant, marked with green quiet zone signage
- Fable World Parade involves amplified music and crowd noise — sensory kits (ear defenders, sunglasses) available on loan from Guest Services

## Stroller Parking

Dedicated stroller parking bays available at Dragon Flight entrance and in the town square adjacent to the Fable Bites kiosk.

## Companion Pass

Available free of charge at Guest Services. Entitles one companion to board alongside the guest at no additional ticket cost on Dragon Flight.

## First Aid

Nearest first aid station: Fable World town square, adjacent to Lady Fable's Emporium.
