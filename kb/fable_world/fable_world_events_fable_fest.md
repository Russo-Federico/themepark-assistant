# Fable World — Fable Fest (Seasonal Event)

## Overview

Fable Fest is a seasonal Halloween event exclusive to Fable World, running from **October 1 to November 2**. During this window, Fable World — Epic Worlds' fairy-tale and fantasy-themed area — transforms into a fall/autumn/Halloween-themed experience. Guests may also refer to this as the "Halloween event," the "fall festival," or the "scare zone event." This event does not affect Future World or Adventure World.

## What Changes in Fable World During Fable Fest

- **Themed decorations:** Fable World's streets, facades, and town square are redecorated with Halloween/autumn theming for the duration of the event.
- **Costumed characters:** additional costumed characters appear throughout Fable World alongside the area's usual cast (e.g. Lady Fable).
- **Scare zones:** two exclusive scare zones are set up within Fable World. These are haunted-attraction-style walkthrough experiences and are **not suitable for guests under 12**.

## Scare Zone Ticketing

- Access to the two Fable Fest scare zones requires a **special event ticket add-on, priced at €10 per person**, on top of standard park admission.
- Standard Fable World attractions (Dragon Flight, Fable World Carousel) remain included in normal park admission during Fable Fest and do not require the scare zone add-on.

## Notes

- Fable Fest is unrelated to Summer Nights (park-wide, June–August) and Future World Innovation Days (Future World only, March) — see `epic_worlds_events_summer_nights.md` and `future_world_events_innovation_days.md` for those separate events.
- Fable World's regular daily entertainment schedule (parade, Dragon Flight story show) continues to run during Fable Fest — see `fable_world_events_daily.md`.
